The Cantonal Police Units

There are 55 cantonal police units in Imperial Royal Union, for the 55 Cantons and Port Imperial Capital Territory, of which a single cantonal police service may be shared between 2 Cantons while some Cantons with large population sizes may have 2 cantonal police units. The cantonal police units are responsible for law enforcement and criminal investigations within their respective canton where their jurisdictional authority is limited to. Each cantonal police unit is headed by a Chief of Police, who reports to both the Directorate-General of Public Security and their respective Cantonal Governments.

Paramilitary Reserves Command (PARACOM)

Formerly constituted as the Imperial Gendarmerie, the Paramilitary Reserves Command is responsible for providing military support services to both the armed forces and civilian public order organisations. It consists of 6 constituent organisations, each reporting to the Chief of Paramilitary Forces.


Military Justice Tribunal

The Military Justice Tribunal manages the military justice system of the armed forces. In times of war, the Military Justice Tribunal is also in charge of the management of the civilian justice system in occupied territories.

Judge Advocate General | ADM William Myer


Military Police Command

The Military Police Command is in charge of policing the armed forces and ensuring security and discipline at all levels. Military personnel are first placed under the custody of the Military Police Command before handing over to the Military Justice Tribunal. The Military Police Command is also responsible for the policing of foreign military personnel in Imperial Royal Union.

Commander, Military Police | FM Gilbert Dieschbourg


Defence Investigations Agency (DIA)

The Defence Investigations Agency is responsible for all criminal investigations involving military personnel and concerning classified military information. The Agency has full authority to arrest and detain military personnel, but must coordinate such operations with the Directorate-General of Public Security for the arrest of civilians. Depending on the case, persons under the custody of the Agency may be handed over to the Military Police Command or the Directorate-General of Public Security.

Director, DIA | VADM Erich Dudley


Occupational Secret Police Corps (OSP)

The Occupational Secret Police Corps is the policing authority in territories occupied by Imperial Royal Union. Responsible for civil-military relations, prisoners of war and displaced persons are also under the purview of the OSP.

Corps Commander, OSP | VADM Aloysius Brunhart


Imperialean Civic Guard Corps (CIVCORPS)

The Imperialean Civic Guard Corps performs civic guard duties and engages in a role primarily in public safety. The Interior Troops under CIVCORPS are paramilitary forces designed to contain large scale insurgencies, or to augment disaster response units in providing order and humanitarian relief, while the special forces under the Joint Public Safety Command provide additional strike capabilities to assist public security forces.

Corps Commander, CIVCORPS | AG Doris Possignol


Special Protection Corps (PROCORPS)

The Special Protection Corps is responsible for the security of all military infrastructure in Imperial Royal Union, inclusive of army camps, naval bases and other defence installations. It also provides armed security officers to key memebrs of the military leadership, or generally all officers with a 3-star ranking and above. Where diplomatic missions of Imperial Royal Union may be located in conflict zones and areas of instability, PROCORPS is also responsible for providing additional security as well as evacuation teams for diplomatic and consular staff of Imperial Royal Union where regular, conventional forces may not be appropriate.

Corps Commander, PROCORPS | AG Julius Cambor

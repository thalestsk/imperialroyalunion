Universal Defence Command (UNICOM)

The Universal Defence Command of the Imperialean National Armed Forces comprises a sizeable proportion of the nation's active military personnel, and is the central operational authority for the armed forces. Headed by the Joint Chief of Defence Staff, UNICOM consists of 5 Operational Commands and the Joint Defence Staff Board.


Joint Defence Staff Board

Chief of Joint Staff

Fältmarskalken

Wilson Lemaire

The Joint Defence Staff Board is the highest-level staff organisation within the armed forces, and supports the operational needs of the armed forces through accurate planning and resource management, often in tandem with its civilian counterpart, the National Defence Administration. It is headed by the Chief of Joint Staff.


Stravanska Strategic Command (STRATCOM)

Commander, STRATCOM

Fältmarskalken

Sigmund Vintersen

Headquartered in the Stravanska Military Base, the Stravanska Strategic Command is the coordinator of the armed forces' special forces as well as strategic assets. This includes control over nuclear missiles, bombers and the ballistic missile submarines deployed around the world. It is also responsible for providing the intermediary connection between the Grande Palais and the various military commands. STRATCOM is headed by the Commander, STRATCOM.


Homeland Defence Command (HOMECOM)

Commander, HOMECOM

Fältmarskalken

Hans Peter Mitterlehner

The Homeland Defence Command is the primary responder to threats against national defence, with its mission centred on protecting border integrity and sovereignty of the nation's lands, airspace and continenal shelf. It is also in charge of the coast guard as well as the nation's ballistic missile defences. Like STRATCOM, HOMECOM receives priority in communications. HOMECOM is headed by the Commander, HOMECOM.


Force Projection Commands

The Force Projection Commands are combined arms elements across the entire armed forces and are responsible for rapid mobilisation and immediate deployment globally at the outbreak of war. The Force Projection Commands are directional, each with its own objective, consisting of the Islandus Force Projection Command (ISCOM), Imperialia Force Projection Command (CONCOM) and the Southern Force Projection Command (SUDCOM). ISCOM is responsible for operating in theatres northwest and west of Imperial Royal Union, while CONCOM is responsible for operating east of Imperial Royal Union, primarily in continental Leloux. Both ISCOM and CONCOM are responsible for the north of Imperial Royal Union. SUDCOM is thus responsible for Imperial Royal Union's south, and more importantly, Antarctic access and control.

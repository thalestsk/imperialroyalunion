Grand Imperial Army

The Grand Imperial Army is the land component of the Imperialean National Armed Forces. Apart from units attached to the Universal Defence Command and Paramilitary Reserves Command, the Army retains operational authority over its land force, maritime support force and air force. Within the Army, which is headed by the Chief of General Staff, the Army High Command is responsible for the operational forces of the Army, while the Army Reserves Command is responsible for the reserves and the army mobilisation system. The General Staff Board is responsible for the overall development and warfighting doctrine of the Army, and assists the Chief of General Staff in the management of the Army.

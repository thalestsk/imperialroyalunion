The Department of Foreign Affairs

The Department of Foreign Affairs is an executive organisation of the Sovereign Affairs Council of HIM Government responsible for Imperial Royal Union's foreign policy, diplomatic relations and global interests. It is headed by the Secretary of Foreign Affairs, who reports to the Lord Minister in the Privy Council of Livenbleaux. The Department is split into 5 distinct divisions - the Policy Secretariat, the Bureau of Consular Services, the Foreign Office, the Imperialean Diplomatic Corps and the Protocol Office.

Scope & Responsibilities

Foreign Office

The Foreign Office is the central point of contact between diplomatic missions within Imperial Royal Union and HIM Government. It is responsible for meeting and summoning ambassadors, hosting of foreign dignitaries and receiving of state gifts and guests amongst its other duties.

Protocol Office

The Protocol Office is responsible for assisting the Department in facilitating trips to overseas nations and trips by foreign dignitaries. It is also responsible for accreditation, courtesies, privileges and immunities for foreign diplomats.

Imperialean Diplomatic Corps

The Imperialean Diplomatic Corps consists of the delegates and officials assigned to the numerous diplomatic missions of Imperial Royal Union overseas, and is responsible for the training and development of career diplomats, as well as the operation and management of such diplomatic missions worldwide.

Bureau of Consular Services

The Bureau of Consular Services operates and manages the consular services within the diplomatic missions of Imperial Royal Union. As the provider of consular services, it is also responsible for liaising with users and administrators of affiliated services from education to immigration.

Policy Secretariat

The Policy Secretariat is an essential part of the Department as it prepares and assists the Secretary of Foreign Affairs through the provision of critical information and updates from across the globe. It is responsible for the compilation of information and intelligence dossiers, including analyses and evaluations as well as the planning of intitiatives to further the interests of Imperial Royal Union globally.

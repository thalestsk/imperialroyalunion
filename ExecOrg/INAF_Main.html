In 2016, the Greater Arms of Imperial Royal Union were reconstituted as the Imperialean National Armed Forces, which is now the military branch of HIM Government. It consists of 4 component forces - the Universal Defence Command, the Paramilitary Reserves Command, the Grand Imperial Navy and the Grand Imperial Army. The INAF is headed by the Chief of Combined Armed Forces, and employs a force of up to 15,000,000 full-time active-duty personnel, with a reserve force of 27,000,000 reservists, volunteers and auxiliaries, located in military bases across Imperial Royal Union, her overseas territories and allied territories. Unlike conventional armed forces, the air force is considered an integrated part of the Navy and Army.

The Austral Community

The Austral Community is a supranational organisation comprising of 6 member states located on Leloux Australis in the Crown Federation of Recon. It is both a political and economic bloc which aims to drive cooperation and development between and within its member states, building on interdependency and sustainable economic growth. The Austral Community is also colloquially referred to as the "AC".

The AC traces its origin to its predecessor, the Continental Prosperity Association, which was dissolved following the annexation of Pelogrinne by Imperial Royal Union. Fundamentally, the Association was unfairly favourable for Pelogrinne although its economic activity and population ranked far lesser than the other member states. The AC sought to level the playing field and ensure fairness on global trade terms and promotes a culture of mutual respect and understanding, in contrast to the confrontational nature in which the Association was set up on.

The AC also established supranational institutions to further promote its objectives, which help the AC coordinate its policies and implement its joint regulations adopted by the member states. Apart from the delegation to the AC, member states also participate in the rotating presidency of the AC, a primarily ceremonial position with limited powers within the AC.

2020 Change of Command & Military Appointments

10 March 2020, Tuesday

  1. A total of 692 new officer cadets received their official commission from His Imperial Majesty Emperor Thales XIII through the service heads of the Grand Imperial Navy and the Grand Imperial Army, AS Catherine Paley and GS Frans-Malte Raeder respectively, of which, 13 were Sword of Honour recipients, to be presented by the Chief of Combined Armed Force GA Stralsund von Leib in the presence of His Imperial Majesty on 15 March 2020.
  2. The Chief of Combined Armed Forces GA Stralsund von Leib presided over the Change of Command Ceremony for the Paramilitary Reserves Command, in which command was handed over from outgoing Chief of Paramilitary Forces AS Florin Codreanu to incoming FM Gilbert Dieschbourg. AS Florin Codreanu will step down and retire from service after 37 years in the Imperialean National Armed Forces. Imperial Royal Union thanks him for his service.
  3. Incoming Chief of Paramilitary Forces FM Gilbert Dieschbourg will be promoted to the rank of Generalissimo (GS), effective 1 April 2020. He will step down from his current appointment as Commander, Military Police, effective 10 March 2020.

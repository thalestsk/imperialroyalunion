Official Statement on the 2019 Novel Coronavirus Outbreak

3 February 2020, Monday

  1. With effect from 0000 hours on 4 February 2020, visitors who have travelled to Mainland China within the prior 21 days shall be denied entry in or transit through Imperial Royal Union. Short-Term Visas by holders of Chinese passports yet to arrive in Imperial Royal Union will be revoked. Imperialean Citizens and Permanent Residents who have travelled to Mainland China within the prior 21 days are to be subjected to quarantine by the National Quarantine & Vaccination Agency at government quarantine facilities.
  2. The Imperialean National Armed Forces will dispatch a total of four aircraft, two on 5 February 2020 and two on 6 February 2020 to evacuate all Imperialean Citizens and Permanent Residents from Mainland China. Upon arrival in Imperial Royal Union, evacuees will be transported to the nearest government quarantine facilities.
  3. A travel advisory to defer all travel to Mainland China has been issued by the Department of Foreign Affairs.
  4. Temperature taking, contact tracing and travel history screening are already pre-existing measures enforced at all border checkpoints by the Border Protection Department and Customs & Excise Agency of the Directorate-General of Public Security in conjunction with the National Quarantine & Vaccination Agency. No further measures are to be expected at border checkpoints.

———

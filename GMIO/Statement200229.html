Update on the Coronavirus Disease 2019

29 February 2020, Saturday

  1. The National Quarantine & Vaccination Agency will extend denial of entry or transit through Imperial Royal Union to all visitors who have travelled to Mainland China, Hong Kong, Macau, Japan, South Korea, Italy and Iran within the prior 21 days with effect from 0000 hours on 1 March 2020. Short Term Visas granted to holders of passports of the above nationalities will be revoked until further notice.
  2. Imperial Royal Union has a total of 28 confirmed cases of the Coronavirus Disease 2019. All of them have a recent travel history to the affected areas and are currently receiving treatment at medical facilities across the nation. A total of 1,463 persons have been quarantined during this period and apart from the confirmed cases, have completed their quarantine.
  3. The National Justice Administration will press charges against 2 South Koreans under Section 34 of the Penal Code (Cap. 99i>) for refusing to comply with contact tracing and quarantine measures upon arrival in Imperial Royal Union and Section 2 of the Enlightenment Act (Cap. 6) for attempting to spread religious beliefs in Imperial Royal Union. They are currently under further investigation by the Directorate-General of Public Security for alleged involvement with the Shincheonji cult in South Korea.

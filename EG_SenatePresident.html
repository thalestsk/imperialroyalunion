The President of the Senate

The President of the Senate is appointed by the Emperor and is also a member of the Privy Council of Livenbleaux. The President is responsible for organising the agenda of and maintaining order in the Senate. The President is assisted by the First Vice-President and the Second Vice-President, who are also appointed by the Emperor and also serve as whips in the Senate to ensure that Senators vote responsibly.

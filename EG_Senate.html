As the upper house of the Estates-General, the Senate has the authority to originate and pass legislation on statutory law in Imperial Royal Union [S(11)(1) of the Instrument of Government 2019 (Cap. 1)]. It is chaired by the President of the Senate. Senators are appointed by the Emperor at his discretion and hold the appointment upon revocation by the Emperor or upon resignation. The Senate meets in the Hall of Infinity within the Grande Palais.

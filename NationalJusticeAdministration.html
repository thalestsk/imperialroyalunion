About

The National Justice Administration is the legal arm of HIM Government. It is responsible for the prosecution of all criminal offences across the nation in the courts, as well as providing legal advice for the government on matters of international and civil law, where the Administration actively pursues legal challenges against local authorities while defending against such challenges from other third parties and also foreign governments in international courts.

Mr. Glenn Sauber, LB
Attorney-General

Leadership

The Administration is headed by the Attorney-General. He is in turn assisted by the Solicitor-General, the Deputy Solicitor-General and the Associate Solicitor-General. The Attorney-General is nominated by the Chief Permanent Secretary of the Public Service Secretariat at the recommendation of the Permanent Secretary for The Interior and appointed by the Emperor.

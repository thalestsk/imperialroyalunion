ISR Cap. 3
In Force

Territorial Domain Act

  1. Short title and commencement
    1. This Act may be cited as the "Territorial Domain Act".
    2. The following provisions of this Act are effective as of 10 April 2021.
  2. Interior Territories
    1. The lands falling within the geographical jurisdiction of the Port Imperial Capital Territory and Territories managed by a Territorial Administrative Authority shall constitute the Interior Territories.
  3. Exterior Territories
    1. The lands falling within the geographical jurisdiction of any of the Imperial Royal Union Overseas Territories or occupied by a permanent Imperialean settlement outside of the Interior Territories shall constitute the Exterior Territories.
  4. Territorial Waters
    1. Any body of water, partial or full, shall constitute the Territorial Waters if the body of water:
      1. is fully enclosed or surrounded by the lands constituting the Interior Territories and Exterior Territories;
      2. extends 18 nautical miles from lands that constitute the Interior Territories and Exterior Territories but is not fully enclosed or surrounded as such; or
      3. shares at least 2 non-contiguous coastal borders with lands situated on the same continental shelf and constituting the Interior Territories and Exterior Territories but is not fully enclosed or surrounded as such.
  5. Exclusive Economic Zone
    1. The areas outside the Interior Territories and Exterior Territories, extending beyond the Territorial Waters up to a limit of 250 nautical miles shall constitute the Exclusive Economic Zone.
  6. Contiguous Domain
    1. The areas constituting the Interior Territories, Exterior Territories and Territorial Waters shall constitute the Contiguous Domain, which shall, by extension, include the airspace above it as a constituent component.
  7. Sovereign Territory of Imperial Royal Union
    1. The Contiguous Domain and the Exclusive Economic Zone, and any areas demarcated on the Official Map of Imperial Royal Union shall constitute the inviolable and sacrosanct international boundary of Imperial Royal Union whereby the sovereignty of Imperial Royal Union has since been exercised with the Emperor of Imperial Royal Union as the true and rightful sovereign and by extension, only the laws of Imperial Royal Union shall be applicable.
  8. Secretary of Domain Affairs
    1. The Secretary of Domain Affairs shall be responsible for promoting and coordinating the promotion of the sovereignty of Imperial Royal Union through external and internal engagements, and the managing of lands outside of the Exterior Territories, through the Department of Domain Affairs & State Lands.
  9. Secretary of Empire Relations
    1. The Secretary of Empire Relations shall be responsible for managing the External Territories of Imperial Royal Union, and promoting the enlargement and expansion of the External Territories, through the Department of Domain Enlargement & Empire Relations.
  10. Overseas Territories
    1. The Overseas Territories of Imperial Royal Union, for the purposes of government efficacy, shall be privileged with the right to self-government with the creation of an Organic Law by Decree. The Organic Law shall be the fundamental law of the Overseas Territory and take precedence over other laws instituted within the Overseas Territory under self-government. Amendment and final interpretation of the Organic Law lies with the Estates-General.
    2. Unless explicitly stated, implied, referred to or promulgated as such, laws of Imperial Royal Union, regardless of precedence, shall not be applicable to Overseas Territories.
    3. Overseas Territories may be established as Special Administrative Departments or Autonomous Overseas Regions, and shall be suffixed with “SAD” or “AOR” respectively, followed by “Imperial Royal Union”, when an official reference to the Overseas Territory is necessary.
    4. Overseas Territories shall return a portion of government revenue to HIM Government.
  11. Special Administrative Departments
    1. The Government of a Special Administrative Department comprises of:
      1. the Emperor of Imperial Royal Union as head-of-state and head-of-government;
      2. the Secretary of Empire Relations and the Chief Executive of the Special Administrative Department as co-deputy heads-of-government;
      3. the Special Administration as the collective term for the executive branch under the Chief Executive;
      4. the Legislative Assembly as the unicameral legislative branch; and
      5. the Superior Court as the judicial branch under the Chief Justice of the Special Administrative Department.
    2. The right to self-government within Special Administrative Departments shall exclude matters relating to foreign affairs, defence, immigration and security, unless authorised by HIM Government.
    3. The Chief Executive shall be appointed by the Estates-General.
  12. Autonomous Overseas Regions
    1. The Government of an Autonomous Overseas Region comprises of:
      1. the Emperor of Imperial Royal Union as head-of-state and head-of-government;
      2. the First Minister of the Autonomous Overseas Region as deputy head-of-government;
      3. the Cabinet as the collective term for the executive branch under the First Minister;
      4. the Parliament as the bicameral legislative branch; and
      5. the Supreme Court as the judicial branch under the Chief Justice of the Autonomous Overseas Region.
    2. The right to self-government within Autonomous Overseas Regions shall exclude matters relating to foreign affairs and defence.
    3. Autonomous Overseas Regions may appoint 2 representatives to the Estates-General, with privileges equal to that of Estates Deputies for representation within HIM Government.
    4. Autonomous Overseas Regions shall only be established on a continental shelf different to that of the Interior Territories.

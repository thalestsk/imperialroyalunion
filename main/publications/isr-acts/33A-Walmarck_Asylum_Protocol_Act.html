ISR Cap. 33A
In Force

Walmarck Asylum Protocol Act

  1. Short title and commencement
    1. This Act may be cited as the "Walmarck Asylum Protocol Act".
    2. The following provisions of this Act are effective as of 14 August 2022.
  2. Ratification
    1. The provisions of the Walmarck Asylum Protocol as agreed by the signatory parties of the Second Atheistic Empire of Imperial Royal Union, the Grand Duchy of Alephescia, the Confederation of Alus and Waldronia, and the Federated States of Walmarck shall hereby be accepted and ratified, and shall hereby be supplemented by the provisions deemed necessary for the expedient and effective implementation of the provisions of the ratified Protocol.
  3. Provisions in force
    1. Where any provision of this Act may appear or be construed to be contrary or be of the contrary intention to other immigration provisions in force, be it explicit or implicit, the such other immigration provisions shall take precedence.
    2. The provisions of this Act apply to persons classified as "prohibited immigrants" under section 15 of the Immigration Act (Cap. 15), unless otherwise stated.
  4. Conditions of entry by air
    1. Every air transport operator shall be responsible for ensuring that arriving air passengers on international flights operated by such operators have the necessary documentation as may be required for entry into Imperial Royal Union.
    2. Every air transport operator shall bear the costs of removal by means of a return flight of arriving air passengers found to not be in compliance of the entry requirements into Imperial Royal Union, in which the costs may be passed on to the non-compliant arriving air passenger at the discretion of the air transport operator, subject to commercial provisions in force regulating the pricing and routing option of the return flight, if any.
    3. The Director of Immigration ("Director") shall impose a fine of IRE 10,000 on any air transport operator that is unable to fulfil such a responsibility for every non-compliant arriving air passenger.

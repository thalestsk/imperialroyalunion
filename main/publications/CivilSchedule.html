Civil Schedule

The Civil Schedule is a unified standard that differentiates the grades and their corresponding remuneration of civil servants within HIM Government, the Territorial Administrative Authorities, and the Cantonal Governments.

From Rank-Grade 7-III and below, the actual appointment titles may vary depending on historical usage or departmental function, although the remuneration and order of precedence of civil servants serving in departments requiring civil servants of such Rank-Grade commensurate with the specified Rank-Grade structure as follows.

The individual Privy Council Executive Organisations and Organs of State each have their own grade structures that commensurate with the Civil Schedule.

Government Media & Information Office

Bureau de presse du gouvernement | Regeringens presskontor

The Government Media & Information Office is the official press and media unit of HIM Government responsible for press briefs and public statements. The merger of all press, information and spokesperson units of every branch and department of HIM Government culminated into the institutionalisation of the Office and serves as the primary point of public access to official reports, statements and releases of HIM Government.


Our Organisation

There are 3 units within the Office reporting to the Director of Communications - the Support Staff Unit, the Press & Media Unit and the Information Unit. The Support Staff Unit handles all ancillary issues arising out of the work of the other 2 units, while the Press & Media Unit handles the GMIO Spokesperson Service and is responsible for all public releases. Before information is released, the Information Unit ensures that sufficient information to prevent misunderstanding or misinformation is published, while also ensuring that protected and confidential information remains redacted.

GMIO Spokesperson Service

The GMIO Spokesperson Service is responsible for HIM Government's weekly press briefing on Friday afternoons. As the primary outward-facing service shared by the whole of HIM Government, the Service ensures that press releases are cohesive, coherent and carefully curated in order to ensure that any response by HIM Government is perceived as united and consistent. Ad-hoc press briefings, when scheduled, are handled by the Chief Spokesperson or the Coordinating Spokespersons while the weekly press briefings are handled by Spokespersons.

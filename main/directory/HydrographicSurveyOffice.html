Hydrographic Survey Office

Bureau des levés hydrographiques | Hydrografikontoret

The Hydrographic Survey Office is an Executive Support Office of HIM Government responsible for the survey, research, analysis, and geophysical management of water bodies within Imperial Royal Union. This includes the provision of navigational aids, bathymetric surveys, tidal information, and research data on the various ecosystems of freshwater and saltwater systems in the nation. The work of the Office serves an important basis for other subordinate departments of HIM government to develop policiesand regulations for the effective management of resources and built-environment assets within, under, and above such water bodies.

Management Department

Département de gestion | Ledningsavdelning

The Management Department is responsible for the management but not the procurement of assets and property of HIM Government. The real estate and the vehicles within the motor pool of HIM Government (with the exception of vehicles registered under the Privy Council Executive Organisations), and the maintenance and management thereof, is thus under the care of the Department.

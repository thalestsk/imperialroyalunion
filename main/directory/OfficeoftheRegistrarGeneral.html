Office of the Registrar-General

Bureau du registraire général | Registreringskontoret

The Office of the Registrar-General is responsible for the maintenance of public records and the update of the government gazette. The official government gazette consists of the Public Registers, Statutory Registers and Judiciary Registers, all of which are maintained by the Office on the IRU E-Gazette Portal in conjunction with the Government Media & Information Office.

Further Information

Public Registers

Public Registers are official public records of private individual registrations or declarations with HIM Government. Examples are:

  • Archive of Declaration of Assets and Interests by Estates Deputies
  • Register of Commercial Entities
  • Elector Rolls of Eligible Voters
  • Register of Marriages
  • Vehicular License Registry

Statutory Registers

Statutory Registers are official public records of primary legislation, subsidiary legislation and from time to time, notices and circulars.

  • IRU Statutory Register
  • Joint Register of Cantonal Statutes
  • Regulations & Notices Register

Judiciary Registers

Judiciary Registers are the official court records of court proceedings. Access to the Judiciary Registers is limited on a paid subscription basis by registered legal practice business entities.

  • Subordinate Courts Registry
  • Supreme Court of Judicature Registry
  • Arbitration & Resolution Registry

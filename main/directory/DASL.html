Department of Domain Affairs & State Lands

Département du domaine territorial | Avdelning för domänadministration

The Department of Domain Affairs & State Lands is responsible for the general administration of the administrative divisions oif Imperial Royal Union. It supports the work of the Territorial Administrative Authorites and liaises with the Lord Administrators and the Governor-Generals of the 55 Cantons of Imperial Royal Union on administrative and legal affairs. The Department is also responsible for the national land sales and land lease programme and ensures that the Territorial Administrative Authorities and Cantonal Governments use, develop, lease or sell land in a responsible and sustainable manner.

Further Information

Liaison Offices

All Territorial Administrative Authorities and Cantonal Governments have a dedicated liaison office within the Department for ensuring that information is well-received on both ends and that the needs and plans of the Territorial Administrative Authorities and Cantonal Governments are communicated properly. Liaison offices also communicate guidance and advice from HIM Government. The appointment of the heads of the Cantonal Administrative Services is decided upon by the Secretary of Domain Affairs after consultation with the liaison offices.

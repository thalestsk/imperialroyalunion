Office of the Procurer-General

Bureau des marchés publics | Upphandlingskontoret

The Office of the Procurer-General is an Executive Support Office of HIM Government responsible for the procurement of equipment and supplies for the State through the operation of the Contracts and Tenders System (CONTENDERS), a digital procurement platform for HIM Government. All public bodies are required to procure through CONTENDERS. External procurement contracts outside of the system must be approved by the Office. The principal objective of the Office is to ensure a high degree of transparency in the procurement decisions of HIM Government to leverage on economies of scale and reduce government waste. The Office is not responsible for contractual matters not relating to procurement.

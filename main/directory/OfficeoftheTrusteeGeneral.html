Office of the Trustee-General

Bureau du curateur public | Offentligt förvaltarämbete

The Office of the Trustee-General is an Executive Support Office of HIM Government responsible for the provision of custodial services for the estates of deceased persons and compensations for insured torts. The Office administers public estates (estates provided by the State) according to statutory law, and administers private estates (personal estates of the deceased) according to wills that have been granted probate, as well as insured torts (particularly in the case of motor vehicle accidents). As such, the Office serves a primarily supervisory role, occasionally being involved in the arbitration for out-of-court settlements. The Office is often used as a measure for the reasonableness of tort claims from the perspective of the government.

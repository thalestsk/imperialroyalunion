About Us

Policy Division

The Policy Division researches and reviews the criminal systems, judicial systems and extradition policies of the nations of the world and develops the extradition policy. Aside from proposing new policies to the Commissioner, it also reviews and recommends changes to existing extradition agreements.

Appeals Division

The Appeals Division receives extradition requests for and appeals against extradition of incarcerated or detained persons in the nation. The findings of the Division are referred to the Commissioners for deliberation.

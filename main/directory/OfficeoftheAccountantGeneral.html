Office of the Accountant-General

Bureau de la comptabilité du gouvernement | Statens redovisningsbyrå

The Office of the Accountant-General is an Executive Support Office of HIM Government responsible for accounting of public sector finance, from the subordinate departments of HIM Government, the Territorial Administrative Authorities, the Cantonal Governments, and the associated corporate entities. It prepares the annual financial statements for HIM Government and determines the regulatory standards for accounting practices for both the public and private sector. Additionally, the Office also provides analytical data of asset movements in the treasury and provides advice on the structuring of such assets.

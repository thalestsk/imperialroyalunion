The Organisation Department is the centralised human resources department-at-large for the recruitment, promotion and development of career civil servants for the Public Service Secretariat. It is responsible for the review of establishment requirements and future career arrangements of all public service officers under its care, and policy work such as the mandatory retirement age and the civil schedule.

The Protective Security Service is a homeland security service subordinated to the Directorate-General of Public Security responsible for providing close protection to senior officials of HIM Government and vulnerable witnesses to key national and transnational criminal acitivities.


About Us

The Protective Security Service is split into 4 operational departments - the Government Security Department, the Close Protection Department, the Overseas Missions Department and the Witness Protection Department.

Director's Office

The Director's Office manages the Protective Security Service and serves as a coordinating body for intragovernmental joint operations.

Government Security Department

The Government Security Department is in charge of all uniformed officers of the Service, who provide armed security to all at-risk facilities, buildings and installations of HIM Government. It is also responsible for maintaining and inspecting the necessary security systems for such facilities.

  • Senior Assistant Director, Government Security | AC Louis Costner (Mr.)
    • Assistant Director, Armed Security | DAC Freya Everdeen (Ms.)
    • Assistant Director, Security Systems | DAC Luca Marconi (Mr.)

Close Protection Department

The Close Protection Department is in charge of all close protection officers assigned to senior officials within HIM Government. Depending on seniority and risk level, the size of the security detail is varied accordingly. The Close Protection Department is not responsible for the security of the Emperor and the Royal Family, which falls under the purview of HIM Special Branch.

  • Senior Assistant Director, Close Protection | AC Karina Dillon (Ms.)

Overseas Missions Department

The Overseas Missions Department is responsible for the security of Imperial Royal Union Diplomatic Missions and Permanent Representations abroad, providing close protection to senior diplomats overseas and maintaining a security presence at the offices. The security response by the Overseas Missions Department in the event of an imminent threat may be augmented by additional officers from the Directorate-General of State Security and the Imperialean National Armed Forces.

  • Senior Assistant Director, Overseas Missions | AC Evan Wichita (Mr.)

    • Assistant Director, Exigent Evacuation Force | DAC Simon Ariston (Mr.)

Witness Protection Department

The Witness Protection Department is responsible for protecting all key witnesses identified as vulnerable to imminent threats. Witnesses enrolled in the Witness Protection Programme receive new identities at the conclusion of trial, and depending on the risk level, the Witness Protection Department may adjust the scope and term of protection accordingly.

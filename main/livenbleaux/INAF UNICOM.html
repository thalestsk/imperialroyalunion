Universal Defence Command

Main Article: Imperialean National Armed Forces

The Universal Defence Command (UNICOM) is the overarching coordinating command authority over the active Operational Commands of the Imperialean National Armed Forces, and the highest military staff authority instituted into the Joint Defence Staff Board. UNICOM is under the command of the Joint Chief of Defence Staff, currently held by GS Colin Haraldsen.


Joint Chief of Defence Staff

The Joint Chief of Defence Staff ("JCDS") is 1 of the 4 senior military officers of a 4-star rank, second only to the Chief of Combined Armed Forces ("CCAF"). The JCDS is nominated by the CCAF, who is subsequently confirmed at the discretion of the Emperor. Unofficially, the JCDS is the most likely to become the CCAF upon the retirement of the incumbent CCAF.

Subordinate Commands

Subordinated to UNICOM are 5 Operational Commands, of which 3 are the Force Projection Commands, with the other 2 being the Stravanska Strategic Command and the Homeland Defence Command, as well as the Joint Defence Staff Board.

Joint Defence Staff Board

The Joint Defence Staff Board is the highest-level staff organisation within the armed forces, and supports the operational needs of the armed forces through accurate planning and resource management, often in tandem with its civilian counterpart, the National Defence Administration. The training and policy development functions of vocational departments such as supply, maintenance and military medicine for the armed forces as a whole are subordinated to the Joint Defence Staff Board. It is headed by the Chief of Joint Staff.

Stravanska Strategic Command (STRATCOM)

Headquartered in the Stravanska Defence Establishment, the Stravanska Strategic Command is the coordinator of the armed forces' special forces as well as strategic assets. This includes control over nuclear missiles, nuclear-armed bombers and the ballistic missile submarines deployed around the world. It is also responsible for providing the intermediary connection between the Grande Palais and the various military commands. STRATCOM is headed by the Commander, STRATCOM.

Homeland Defence Command (HOMECOM)

The Homeland Defence Command is the primary responder to threats against national defence and the first line of defence, with its mission centred on protecting border integrity and sovereignty of the nation's lands, airspace and continenal shelf. It is also in charge of the coast guard as well as the nation's ballistic missile defences. HOMECOM is headed by the Commander, HOMECOM.

Islandus Force Projection Command (ISCOM)

As a Force Projection Command, ISCOM was formed out combined arms elements across the entire armed forces and is responsible for rapid mobilisation and immediate deployment globally at the outbreak of war. The name "ISCOM" is derived from "Islandus Command", a reference to the Territory of Islandus, which serves as the staging area for most of ISCOM's operations. As a result, ISCOM is responsible for operating in theatres northwest and west of Imperial Royal Union.

Imperialia Force Projection Command (CONCOM)

As a Force Projection Command, CONCOM was formed out combined arms elements across the entire armed forces and is responsible for rapid mobilisation and immediate deployment globally at the outbreak of war. The name "CONCOM" is derived from "Continental Command", a reference to Contintental Leloux which Imperial Royal Union is located on. CONCOM is responsible for operating in theatres northeast and east of Imperial Royal Union, primarily on Continental Leloux, and thus has a greater concentration of army units.

Southern Force Projection Command (SUDCOM)

As a Force Projection Command, SUDCOM was formed out combined arms elements across the entire armed forces and is responsible for rapid mobilisation and immediate deployment globally at the outbreak of war. The name "SUDCOM" is derived from "Southern Command" ("Sud" is "South" in French), referencing the direction in which SUDCOM is responsible for. Owing to a lack of conventional threats to the south, SUDCOM is primarily responsible for securing Antarctic access and maritime security in the southerly waters. In times of war, SUDCOM augments the forces of both ISCOM and CONCOM.

Main Article: Imperialean National Armed Forces

The Grand Imperial Navy is the naval branch of the Imperialean National Armed Forces. Apart from units attached to the Universal Defence Command and Paramilitary Reserves Command, the Navy retains operational authority over its naval forces, marines and naval air arm. Within the Navy, which is headed by the Chief of Naval Staff, the Admiralty High Command is responsible for the operational forces of the Navy, while the Naval Reserves Command is responsible for the reserves and the naval mobilisation system. The Naval Staff Board is responsible for the overall development and warfighting doctrine of the Navy, and assists the Chief of Naval Staff in the management of the Navy.


Chief of Naval Staff

The Chief of Naval Staff ("CNS") is 1 of the 4 senior military officers of a 4-star rank, second only to the Chief of Combined Armed Forces ("CCAF"). The CNS is nominated by the CCAF, who is subsequently confirmed at the discretion of the Emperor.

Deputy Chiefs of Naval Staff

There are 3 Deputy Chiefs of Naval Staff ("DCNS") assisting the Chief of Naval Staff, covering Operations (Admiralty High Command), Management (Naval Staff Board), and Reserves (Naval Reserves Command). All 3 DCNS are nominated by both the CCAF and the CNS, who are then subsequently confirmed at the discretion of the Emperor.

Naval Staff Board

The Naval Staff Board comprises of the Deputy Chief of Naval Staff, Management, and the 8 Assistant Chiefs of Naval Staff (2-star rank) at the head of the Naval Staff Organisations. Together, the Naval Staff Board steers the overarching development and doctrine of the Navy.

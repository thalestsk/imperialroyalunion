Imperialean National Armed Forces

The Imperialean National Armed Forces is a privy council executive organisation under the Council of War with command authority derived from the Emperor over the armed forces. At the time of Emperor Thales XIII's accession to the throne, the armed forces were organised as the Greater Arms of Imperial Royal Union, which was then subsequently reformed into the current and more modernised Imperialean National Armed Forces. It is also a privy council executive organisation under the Council of War.


About Us

The Imperialean National Armed Forces is led by the Chief of Combined Armed Forces ("CCAF"; equivalent to 5-star rank) as the chief military advisor to the Emperor, with the Emperor as the Commander-in-Chief of the armed forces. There are 2 major command systems, the Universal Defence Command and the Paramilitary Reserves Command, and 2 service branches, the Grand Imperial Navy and the Grand Imperial Army, falling under the direct oversight of the CCAF. Following the 2015 reorganisation of the armed forces, the air force service branch was absorbed into Navy and Army capabilities, the military police and internal troops were aggregated into the Paramilitary Reserves Command, and the Universal Defence Command took over the operational command duties of the former Greater Arms of Imperial Royal Union Strategic Command.

Underlying Legislation

Commissioned officers, non-commissioned officers and enlisted soldiers of the Imperialean National Armed Forces are bound to the Armed Forces Code, which dervies its powers from the Armed Forces Act. The Armed Forces Act also sets forth the corresponding command authority and enshrines the Emperor's sole right to raise troops.

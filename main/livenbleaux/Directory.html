Directory of Government Agencies

The Directory of Government Agencies is a one-stop e-service for accessing information on all HIM Government affiliated and subordinate departments. Departments are distributed between the different Groups of the Public Service Secretariat for stronger coordination on the implementation of policy. There are 3 classifications of government agencies under HIM Government - Government Commissions, Statutory Departments and Affiliated Public Bodies.

Government Commissions

Technocratic institutions for research, consultation, formulation, and advisory on specialised policy areas and may be assigned with specific oversight duties.

Statutory Departments

Executive departments directly subordinated to the Permanent Secretaries and generally derive most of their executive powers from legislative authority.

Affiliated Public Bodies

Semi-autonomous government-linked bodies with funding and oversight from HIM Government and may operate as for-profit or non-profit organisations.

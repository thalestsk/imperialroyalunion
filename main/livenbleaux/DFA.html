Department of Foreign Affairs

Département des affaires étrangères | Utrikesdepartementet

The Department of Foreign Affairs is a privy council executive organisation under Sovereign Affairs Council responsible for the promotion and furthering of Imperialean national interests on the global stage. As the primary coordinator of diplomacy inside and outside of Imperial Royal Union, it is responsible for the administration of foreign policy instruments of HIM Government.


Secretary of Foreign Affairs

The Secretary of Foreign Affairs is appointed at the discretion of the Emperor, and is responsible for managing the Department of Foreign Affairs and its respective divisions, often regarded as Imperial Royal Union's highest representative and most senior diplomat.


The current Secretary of Foreign Affairs is Ms. Trina Nilsson, who was appointed on 11 November 2017.

Foreign Office

The Foreign Office is the central point of contact between diplomatic missions within Imperial Royal Union and HIM Government. It is responsible for meeting and summoning ambassadors, hosting of foreign dignitaries and receiving of state gifts and guests amongst its other duties.

Protocol Office

The Protocol Office is responsible for assisting the Department in facilitating trips to overseas nations and trips by foreign dignitaries. It is also responsible for accreditation, courtesies, privileges and immunity for foreign diplomats.

Consular Office

The Consular Office operates and manages the consular services within the diplomatic missions of Imperial Royal Union. As the provider of consular services, it is also responsible for liaising with users and administrators of affiliated services from education to immigration. The Consular Office determines the appointment of consular officers to Embassies and Consulates.

Policy Secretariat

The Policy Secretariat is an essential part of the Department as it prepares and assists the Secretary of Foreign Affairs through the provision of critical information and updates from across the globe. It is responsible for the compilation of information and intelligence dossiers, including analyses and evaluations as well as the planning of intitiatives to further the interests of Imperial Royal Union globally.

Diplomatic Institute

The Diplomatic Institute consists of the delegates and officials assigned to the numerous diplomatic missions of Imperial Royal Union overseas, and is responsible for the training and development of career diplomats, as well as the operation and management of such diplomatic missions worldwide. It also serves as the personnel management division of the Department, since all employees of the Department are considered as career diplomats.

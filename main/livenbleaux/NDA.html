National Defence Administration

The National Defence Administration is a privy council executive organisation under the Council of War primarily providing administrative and policy support for the nation's military forces. To ensure the efficacy of the armed forces, the planning and development arm on the national defence policy was separated from the operational arm, thus forming the National Defence Administration, with a secondary purpose of allowing for a higher degree of civilian oversight without unwarranted interference into military operations.


About Us

The National Defence Administration is led by the Director-General of Defence Administration, who is appointed by the Emperor. The Director-General is then in turn responsible for the appointment of all subordinate officers. While the National Defence Administration is a civilian-managed entity and thus only employs civilians, the Director-General may appoint military officers in active service alongside civilian officers to fill only the positions within the divisions of the Krigsmaskin.

The National Service Department is responsible for administering the mass enlistment programme of every physically fit male and female, along with the formulation and review of the national population pool eligible for draft, as well as the administration of matters pertaining to reserve forces and retired forces, including the pension fund and post-service benefits and services for veterans.

The Plans & Transformation Directorate is responsible for the future development of the armed forces. It is a reviewing authority tasked to renew and refine systems and processes within the military, as well as develop new state-of-the-art systems and technology for combat deployment, with the Defence Material Institute managing the indexation system and certification regime for all military equipment and prototypes.

The Krigsmaskin (Swedish for "War Machine") is the planning office and support group for the Chief of War Staff of the Imperialean National Armed Forces. At the outbreak of war, the Krigsmaskin is responsible for transforming the nation to a state of war and steering the war economy.

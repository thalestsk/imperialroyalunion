Paramilitary Reserves Command

Main Article: Imperialean National Armed Forces

The Paramilitary Reserves Command (PARACOM) is the overarching coordinating command authority over all paramilitary and provost forces of the Imperialean National Armed Forces, as well as the components of the former Internal Troops under the Greater Arms of Imperial Royal Union.


Chief of Paramilitary Forces

The Chief of Paramilitary Forces ("CPF") is 1 of the 4 senior military officers of a 4-star rank, second only to the Chief of Combined Armed Forces ("CCAF"). The CPF is nominated by the CCAF, who is subsequently confirmed at the discretion of the Emperor. Prior to the merger of the miltiary provost forces and the civilian paramilitary forces, the CPF was known as the Chief of Gendarmerie. Today, the term "Gendarmerie" has no relation to the nation's paramilitary forces.

Subordinate Commands

Subordinated to PARACOM are 3 Provost Institutions and 3 Paramilitary Corps. PARACOM is the only arm of the Imperialean National Armed Forces with inward-facing responsibilities. Apart from ensuring the discipline of the armed forces through its Provost Institutions, the Paramilitary Corps may be used to augment the force response of local law enforcement or HOMECOM when responding to an invasion or border incursion.

Military Justice Tribunal

The Military Justice Tribunal manages the military justice system of the armed forces, providing legal advice to servicemen and servicewomen as well as legal opinions for military operations. In times of war, the Military Justice Tribunal is also in charge of the management of the civilian justice system in occupied territories. It is headed by the Judge Advocate General.

Military Police Command

The Military Police Command is in charge of policing the armed forces and ensuring security and discipline at all levels. Military personnel are first placed under the custody of the Military Police Command before handing over to the Military Justice Tribunal. The Military Police Command is also responsible for the policing of foreign military personnel in Imperial Royal Union. It is headed by the Commander, Military Police.

Defence Investigations Agency (DIA)

The Defence Investigations Agency is responsible for all criminal investigations involving military personnel and concerning classified military information. The Agency has full authority to arrest and detain military personnel, but must coordinate such operations with the Directorate-General of Public Security for the arrest of civilians. Depending on the case, persons under the custody of the Agency may be handed over to the Military Police Command or the Directorate-General of Public Security.

Occupational Secret Police Corps (OSP)

The Occupational Secret Police Corps is the policing authority in territories occupied by Imperial Royal Union. It is responsible for civil-military relations, prisoners of war and displaced persons. OSP fulfils the function of prosecutor, judge and executioner in times of emergency in occupied territories where it may be inconvenient or impractical to establish the Military Justice Tribunal or turnover jurisdiction to local law enforcement.

Imperialean Civic Guard Corps (CIVCORPS)

The Imperialean Civic Guard Corps performs civic guard duties and engages in a role primarily in public safety. The Interior Troops under CIVCORPS are paramilitary forces designed to contain large scale insurgencies, or to augment disaster response units in providing order and humanitarian relief, while the special forces under the Joint Public Safety Command provide additional strike capabilities to assist public security forces.

Special Protection Corps (PROCORPS)

The Special Protection Corps is responsible for the security of all military infrastructure in Imperial Royal Union, inclusive of army camps, naval bases and other defence installations. It also provides armed security officers to key members of the military leadership, or generally all officers with a 3-star ranking and above. Where diplomatic missions of Imperial Royal Union may be located in conflict zones and areas of instability, PROCORPS is also responsible for providing additional security as well as evacuation teams for diplomatic and consular staff of Imperial Royal Union where regular, conventional forces may not be appropriate.

Main Article: Imperialean National Armed Forces

The Grand Imperial Army is the land component of the Imperialean National Armed Forces. Apart from units attached to the Universal Defence Command and Paramilitary Reserves Command, the Army retains operational authority over its land force, maritime support force and air force. Within the Army, which is headed by the Chief of General Staff, the Army High Command is responsible for the operational forces of the Army, while the Army Reserves Command is responsible for the reserves and the army mobilisation system. The General Staff Board is responsible for the overall development and warfighting doctrine of the Army, and assists the Chief of General Staff in the management of the Army.


Chief of General Staff

The Chief of General Staff ("CGS") is 1 of the 4 senior military officers of a 4-star rank, second only to the Chief of Combined Armed Forces ("CCAF"). The CGS is nominated by the CCAF, who is subsequently confirmed at the discretion of the Emperor.

Deputy Chiefs of General Staff

There are 3 Deputy Chiefs of General Staff ("DCGS") assisting the Chief of General Staff, covering Operations (Army High Command), Management (General Staff Board), and Reserves (Army Reserves Command). All 3 DCGS are nominated by both the CCAF and the CGS, who are then subsequently confirmed at the discretion of the Emperor.

General Staff Board

The General Staff Board comprises of the Deputy Chief of General Staff, Management, and the 8 Assistant Chiefs of General Staff (2-star rank) at the head of the General Staff Organisations. Together, the General Staff Board steers the overarching development and doctrine of the Army.

Privy Council of Livenbleaux

The Privy Council of Livenbleaux is a council appointed at the sole discretion of the Emperor. Sometimes referred to as the "Inner Cabinet", it meets to discuss matters of the state at the highest level before delegating responsibilities to the subordinate levels of HIM Government. The Privy Council is responsible for the core functions of HIM Government, namely defence, security, foreign affairs and supervision, as well as the coordination of the 3 branches of government. The 3 branches are unified under the Emperor; there is no pretense of separation of powers. Apart from the Emperor as the chairman of the Privy Council, there are 5 other members of the Privy Council.

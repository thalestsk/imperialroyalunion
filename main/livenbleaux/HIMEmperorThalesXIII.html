HIM Emperor Thales XIII

His Imperial Majesty Emperor Thales XIII, born Thales Sanford Kylian on 15 February 1997, ascended the throne on 14 July 2012 upon the death of his father, the late Emperor Thales XII, at the age of 15, becoming the youngest reigning Emperor in the history of the nation and the 18th monarch from the House of Thales. The Grand Duchy of Alephescia recognises the reigning monarch of Imperial Royal Union as its head of state (Grand Duke of Alephescia), and as such the Emperor is also the ex officio Co-Prince of Altessa by virtue of being the Grand Duke of Alephescia.

The Department of the Emperor

The Department of the Emperor is both the private office of the Emperor and the highest national coordinating body on legislative, executive and judicial matters. The Department comprises the Grande Palais Secretariat and the Royal Household Corps.

The Grande Palais Secretariat

The Grande Palais Secretariat houses the officials of the Department of The Emperor as well as the private staff to the Emperor. It is directed by the Director of the Livenbleaux Political Office, Mr. Clive Sigmaren. It is responsible for the welfare and assistance of the Sovereign in his administrative and ceremonial duties, coordinating the Emperor's schedule and accommodating to requests inside and outside of the Grande Palais.

The Royal Household Corps

The Royal Household Corps comprises the officers of the royal household and its administrative body, and also the household guards responsible for the protection of the Emperor and the royal family. The Royal Household Corps is managed by HH Gustav Malinwood, the Lord Grand Commander. The Imperial Guard Division consists of the ceremonial but personal armed guards that are raised within the Royal Household Corps, while the Territorial Garrison Division draws its strength from active units within the Imperialean National Armed Forces' Homeland Defence Command (HOMECOM).

HIM Emperor Thales XIII

His Imperial Majesty Emperor Thales XIII, born Thales Sanford Kylian on 15 February 1997, ascended the throne on 14 July 2012 upon the death of his father, the late Emperor Thales XII, at the age of 15, becoming the youngest reigning Emperor in the history of the nation and the 18th monarch from the House of Thales. The Grand Duchy of Alephescia recognises the reigning monarch of Imperial Royal Union as its head of state (Grand Duke of Alephescia), and as such the Emperor is also the ex officio Co-Prince of Altessa by virtue of being the Grand Duke of Alephescia.

The Department of the Emperor

The Department of the Emperor is both the private office of the Emperor and the highest national coordinating body on legislative, executive and judicial matters. The Department comprises the Grande Palais Secretariat, the Royal Household Administration and the Royal Household Guards.

The Grande Palais Secretariat

The Grande Palais Secretariat houses the officials of the Department of The Emperor as well as the private staff to the Emperor. It is directed by the HIM Principal Private Secretary, His Highness Nichols Limatt. It is responsible for the welfare and assistance of the Sovereign in his administrative and ceremonial duties, coordinating the Emperor's schedule and accommodating to requests inside and outside of the Grande Palais as well as the management of the Royal Household. The Secretariat is comprised of 3 departments, each serving a different need of the Emperor.

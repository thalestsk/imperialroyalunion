The Government Media & Information Office

The Government Media & Information Office is the official press and media unit of HIM Government responsible for press briefs and public statements. Initially, the Office was institutionalised for the purpose of monitoring reporting by the press and ensuring corrections were published accordingly to maintain the accountability and credibility of the national press. At the turn of the decade on 1 January 2020, a decision was made to remove the supervisory functions of the Office and combine the spokespersons of every subordinate department of HIM Government into a single, cohesive information unit. With the exception of Official Statements from the Grande Palais issued personally by the Emperor, the Office is the official mouthpiece of the Emperor and HIM Government. Requests For Information (RFIs) are handled by the Office's Information Unit.

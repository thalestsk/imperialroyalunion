About

The Social Insurance Administration administers the national pension programme. It is responsible for ensuring the submission of mandatory contributions by the stakeholders involved in the pension system, deduction of premiums where authorised, and subsequently the payout of claims made by such stakeholders. The Administration works closely with insurance providers to develop a degree of universal coverage for every working person in Imperial Royal Union, as well as reputable fund houses and investment firms to allow continued capital growth and wealth preservation of national savings.

Leadership

The Administration is headed by the General Administrator of Social Insurance. The General Administrator is in turn assisted by Assistant General Administrators and Chief Regional Administrators.The General Administrator is nominated by the Chief Permanent Secretary of the Public Service Secretariat at the recommendation of the Permanent Secretary for Work & Pensions and appointed by the Emperor.

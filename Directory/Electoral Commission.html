About

The Electoral Commission is responsible for the administration of democratic elections and referendums in Imperial Royal Union. As electoral laws are drafted by and elections organised by the Territorial Administrative Authorities, the Commission is responsible for monitoring the electoral process and validating the results as an independent third-party. The Commission also provides electoral reform advice and acts as an international observer for relevant elections concerning Imperialean interests.


Leadership

The Electoral Commission is headed by the Elector-General, who is appointed directly by the Chief Permanent Secretary. The Elector-General is responsible for the overall duties of the Commission, while the actual supervisory work of the Commission is dealt with by the Electoral Commissioners for the individual Territories.

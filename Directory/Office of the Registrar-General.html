About

The Office of the Registrar-General is responsible for the maintenance of public records and the update of the government gazette. The official government gazette consists of the Public Registers, Statutory Registers and Judiciary Registers, all of which are maintained by the Office. The most notable gazette is the Imperial Royal Union Statutory Register, which contains all Acts of Estates and other related nation legislative instruments of Imperial Royal Union.


Leadership

The Office of the Registrar-General is headed by the Registrar-General, who is appointed by the Lord High Consul at the recommendation of the Chief Permanent Secretary.

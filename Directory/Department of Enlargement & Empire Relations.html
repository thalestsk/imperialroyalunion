About

The Department of Enlargement & Empire Relations is responsible for the liaison between HIM Government and the self-governing overseas territories of Imperial Royal Union. Revenue collection, international representation and local government supervision is administered and handled by the Department. The Department is also responsible for hosting the Continental Prosperity Forum between Imperial Royal Union, Alephescia, Altessa and Alus & Waldronia of Continental Leloux.


Leadership

The Department is headed by the Secretary of Empire Relations, who is appointed by the Lord High Consul in concurrence with the Chief Permanent Secretary and at the recommendation of the Permanent Secretary for The Exterior.

Overseas Territories

There are two types of overseas territories - Autonomous Indigeneous Collectivities (AICs) and Special Administrative Territories (SATs).

AICs have higher devolved authority due to a higher number of resident indigeneous peoples and therefore the reach of HIM Government is relatively limited in AICs. Such powers are denoted within the AICs' Instrument of Self-Government. A unique characteristic of AICs is that an AIC may only be accessed from Imperial Royal Union - the borders of the AICs are therefore completely closed to the rest of the world.

SATs are more restrictive with autonomy - while self-government is granted, the Head of Government is nominated by the Secretary of Empire Relations and confirmed by the Emperor, and the Chamber of Deputies may supersede the local legislature, including suspending the whole legislature. Only the legislatures of the SATs are elected by the resident population. Within SATs, security and military officials of HIM Government may carry out activities with the acknowledgement of the local government.

Both AICs and SATs are to have English as their official language and French and Swedish as their recognised languages. In addition, one local language may also be given the status of an official language. AICs and SATs derive their penal code from Imperial Royal Union, and therefore extradition holds between Imperial Royal Union and its overseas territories.

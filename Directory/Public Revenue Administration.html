About

The Public Revenue Administration is the central tax collection agency of Imperial Royal Union. Unless otherwise specified in legislatiion, almost all taxes, fees, penalties, duties and tariffs collected by HIM Government, the local authorities, and all subordinate departments of HIM Government are turned over to the Administration. The Administration is only responsible for the collection, consolidation and proper accounting of revenue collected, before being passed on to the Central Monetary Authority for safekeeping before the monies are drawn for use under supply legisation. Tax fraud and evasion is handled by the Directorate-General of Public Security's financial crime unit with the National Investigations Agency.

Ms. Nandita Khurshid
Comptroller-General of Revenue

Leadership

The Agency is headed by the Comptroller-General of Revenue. The Comptroller-General is in turn assisted by Assistant Comptroller-Generals. The Comptroller-General is nominated by the Chief Permanent Secretary of the Public Service Secretariat at the recommendation of the Permanent Secretary for Economic Affairs and appointed by the Emperor.

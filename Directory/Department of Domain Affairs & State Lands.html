About

The Department of Domain Affairs & State Lands is responsible for the general administration of the administrative divisions oif Imperial Royal Union. It supports the work of the Territorial Administrative Authorites and liaises with the Lord Administrators and the Governor-Generals of the 55 Cantons of Imperial Royal Union on administrative and legal affairs. A key role of the Department is to ensure that Territorial laws and Cantonal laws are in compliance with the laws passed by the Estates-General or set forth by the Supreme Court of Judicature.


Leadership

The Department is headed by the Secretary of Domain Affairs, who is appointed by the Lord High Consul in concurrence with the Chief Permanent Secretary and at the recommendation of the Permanent Secretary for The Interior.

Administrative Divisions of Imperial Royal Union

Imperial Royal Union consists of 5 Territories - the Port Imperial Capital Territory, the Territory of Islandus, the Territory of Imperialia, the Estern Territory and the Southern Isles Territory. Each Territory, with the exception of Port Imperial Capital Territory, is further sub-divided into Cantons. Territories are managed by a Lord Administrator through a Territorial Administrative Authority, elected by plebiscite. Cantons are the governed by a Governor-General through a Cantonal Government, elected by plebiscite. The Territorial Administrative Authorities hold primarily executive authority, while Cantonal Governments hold both executive and legislative authority, shared by their own Cantonal Legislatures. Altogether, these institutions remain subservient to HIM Government. For Port Imperial Capital Territory, legislative authority rests solely with the Emperor (through Capital Decrees), with the Lord Administrator as the executor.

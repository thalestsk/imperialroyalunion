About

The Organisation Department is a Subordinate Office of the General Secretariat of the Public Service Secretariat. As the human resources department-at-large for the Public Service Secretariat, it is responsible for the promotion and future career arrangements of all public service officers under its care, and policy work such as the mandatory retirement age and the civil schedule.


Leadership

The Organisation Department is headed by the Assistant Chief Permanent Secretary, who is appointed directly by the Chief Permanent Secretary.

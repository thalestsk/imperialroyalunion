About

The Antarctic Affairs & Oceanic Office is the premier authority in Imperial Royal Union for research and land surveyance in the Anatarctic. While Imperial Royal Union adheres to the non-militarisation policy of the Antarctic, the Office is responsible for identifying and declaring areas of interest for Imperial Royal Union, and for ensuring Imperial Royal Union's unfettered access into the Antarctic, be it for civilian or military reasons. As a result, it works closely with the Imperialean National Armed Force's Southern Force Projection Command (SUDCOM) to achieve this goal.


Leadership

The Office is headed by the Director of Antarctic Affairs, who is appointed by the Lord High Consul in concurrence with the Chief Permanent Secretary and at the recommendation of the Permanent Secretary for The Exterior.

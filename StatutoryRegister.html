ISR E-Service

The ISR E-Service provides access to the IRU Statutory Register, which is the official government gazette of HIM Government and consists of all Acts of Estates passed in the Estates-General and other underlying fundamental legislation of Imperial Royal Union. It is maintained by the Office of the Registrar-General.

The Department of The Emperor

The Department of The Emperor is the highest office of Imperial Royal Union and is the central core of HIM Government. The reigning monarch, HIM Emperor Thales XIII, rules Imperial Royal Union through the Department. The Department is located in Chateau Livenbleaux of the Grande Palais.
